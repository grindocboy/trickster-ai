Bid accuracy means your team made its contract without taking 3+ bags.

Bidding: Count aces, protected kings, long spades, and short suits. Do not count every queen.
Partner play: If your partner is winning and you do not need the trick, throw low. Save winners for later.
Bags: Overtricks are useful only when the enemy is set. Otherwise they can turn into a 100-point penalty.
Spades broken: You cannot lead spades until someone cuts with a spade, unless your hand is all spades.

Rules used

  • Partners: South/North vs West/East.
  • First team to 500 wins.
  • Follow suit if possible.
  • Spades cannot be led until broken.
  • Made bid: 10 × bid + bags.
  • Failed bid: −10 × bid.
  • 10 bags: −100 penalty.
  • Nil bids are allowed for you in practice mode; AI currently bids 1–7.
